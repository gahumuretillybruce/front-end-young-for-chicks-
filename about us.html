ABOUT US

Young4ChickS is an urban brooder initiative targeting youth aged 18–30. Founded by a Youth Member of Parliament, it aims to empower young urban dwellers by enabling them to participate in wealth creation through poultry farming. The program supports young farmers by supplying chicks and feeds while maintaining affordable access and sustainable support systems.

MISSION

To stimulate wealth creation and economic involvement among youth through accessible poultry farming opportunities.

We ensure every registered young farmer receives up to 100 chicks (or 500 for returnees) along with feed support to kickstart their agribusiness journey.

VISION

To become a leading youth-centered agricultural empowerment platform in urban settings.

We envision transforming unemployed youth into successful poultry entrepreneurs with the tools, training, and resources they need.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Over 1,200 youth farmers have been supported through the program since its launch, with consistent chick and feed distribution.

The brooder system is fully digitized with request tracking, approval workflows, and a stock management dashboard for transparency.
